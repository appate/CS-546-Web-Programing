My Education

University of California, Berkeley

My favorite class at UC Berkeley was Introduction to Computer Science.I loved learning how to code and create programs that could solve real-world problems.

One memorable memory from UC Berkeley was when I participated in a hackathon and created a web app that helped people find volunteer opportunities in their area.

San Francisco State University

My favorite class at SFSU was Creative Writing.I enjoyed being able to express my thoughts and ideas through writing.

A memorable memory from SFSU was when I joined the school's literary magazine and was able to publish some of my own writing.

City College of San Francisco

My favorite class at CCSF was Psychology.I found it fascinating to learn about human behavior and how the mind works.

A memorable memory from CCSF was when I joined the debate team and had the opportunity to travel to different schools to compete.
